Weight loss group

Join our local weight loss class

At class you will have a weekly private weigh-in with your class leader, who can answer any questions you have.

You will then sit for a class talk, where the leaders cover many different topics. There are many supports througout the week. Text group with all the members of the class. Weekly news letters, with latest information and new recipe ideas.

We are also in the process of creating our very own app. Where you can track your food plan and progress.

Our Plan is called the Balance Plan, based on plan of eating a balanced healthy diet, no food is off limits, but limits and controls are put on the foods that need watching. We have a team of researchers that have come up with this plan and consulted many resourses.
